# E-commerce Checkout Flow

Redesigned the entire checkout process to minimize friction and maximize conversions.

## Improvements Made

- Single-page checkout with progressive disclosure
- Guest checkout option
- Address autocomplete
- Multiple payment integrations

## Results

Cart abandonment dropped from 68% to 33% after implementation.
